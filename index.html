Help

  • Add a Hexagon: Click on the map to add a hexagon at the clicked location.
  • Remove a Hexagon: Click on an existing hexagon to remove it. Make sure the resolution of the hexagon is the same as the resolution slider value.
  • Adjust Hexagon Resolution: Use the resolution slider in the controls section to adjust the resolution of the hexagons.
  • Change Hexagon Color: Use the color picker in the controls section to change the color of the hexagons.
  • Change Hexagon Opacity: Use the opacity slider in the controls section to change the opacity of the hexagons.
  • Measurement Mode: Click the "Start Measurement" button to enter measurement mode. Click once to set the starting point, then move the mouse to see a black dashed measurement line and distance in km displayed prominently at the bottom-center of the screen. Click again to clear the measurement.
  • Save: Click the "Save" button to download both hexagons and partners as a JSON file.
  • Load: Click the "Load" button and select a JSON file to load hexagons and partners.
  • Add Partner: Click the "Add Partner" button to open a form for creating a partner with H3 hexagonal zones.
  • Edit Partner: Click on a partner marker on the map, then click "Edit Partner" to modify its settings.
  • Delete Partner: Click on a partner marker and use "Delete Partner" to remove it.
  • Partner Layer Toggles: Use the toggles in the partner panel to show/hide primary and secondary hexagonal zones.
